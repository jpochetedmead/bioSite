Yismerlin is a dedicated mother, a lifelong learner, and a woman driven by creativity and love for her family. Born and raised in the Dominican Republic, she grew up in a peaceful rural town with her aunt and uncle.

She currently works as a Customer Service Assistant at Hospital Traumatológico Ney Arias Lora and spends her days raising her daughter. She's also committed to learning English to expand her career opportunities.

"I want people to understand what a great mother I am!"
